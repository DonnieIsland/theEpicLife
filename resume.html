About WITHIN

Eric is an ordinary man that has a perfect life. A simple life, yet perfect. He has a gorgeous wife Kim, a secure job, and a beautiful home in New York City, living the American dream. All by the age of 30. All seems so perfect from the outside, but the inside is much different. Something does not feel right. Eric does not yet know what it is, but subconsciously he feels it. Is this seemingly the end of Eric's perfect life? Or an awakening?
